    Why another Bible translation Tool?

    To provide a minimalist USFM editor for Bible Translators to use without any specific training or technical know how .

    What is USFM ?

    USFM stands for Unified Standard Format Markers the de-facto markup convention for Bible Translation software tools .

    More info at http://paratext.org/about/usfm
